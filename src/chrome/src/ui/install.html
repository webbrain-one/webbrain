One small setup step

Keep WebBrain one click away

Your browser may hide newly installed extensions. Keep WebBrain visible so it stays easy to open.

  1. 1
    Open Extensions

    Open your browser’s extension controls near the address bar.

  2. 2
    Keep WebBrain visible

    Find WebBrain and choose the pin, show, or keep-in-toolbar option.

  3. 3
    Open it from any page

    Select the WebBrain icon, or press Alt+Shift+W.

This cannot be done automatically—your browser leaves toolbar choices to you.
